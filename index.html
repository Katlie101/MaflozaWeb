About

What is Mafloza's cleaning co.?

Mafloza's Cleaning Services is a proudly South African cleaning company committed to delivering top-tier, reliable, and eco-friendly cleaning solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial clients. Established in 2024, we have built a reputation based on trust, attention to detail and unmatched customer service.

Our Vision

To become South Africa's most respected and preferred cleaning service provider through innovation, integrity, and consistent customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

To provide professional cleaning services that exceed client expectations by using safe products, well-trained staff, and modern cleaning technology.
